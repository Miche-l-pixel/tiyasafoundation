Refund Policy

Effective Date: 1st March 2026

Back to Home

At Tiyasa Social Welfare Foundation, we are grateful for your generosity and support. All donations made through our website are voluntary contributions made towards our charitable objectives.

1. General Policy

Donations are generally non-refundable, as they are immediately allocated toward ongoing charitable programs and activities.

2. Exceptional Refunds

Refunds may be considered in the following cases:

  • Duplicate transaction
  • Incorrect amount charged due to technical error
  • Unauthorized transaction reported by donor

Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days from the date of transaction.

3. Refund Process

To request a refund, please email tiyasasocialwelfare@gmail.com with the following details:

  • Full Name
  • Transaction ID
  • Donation Date
  • Donation Amount
  • Reason for refund request

After verification, if approved, refunds will be processed through the original mode of payment within 7–10 working days.

4. Decision Authority

Tiyasa Social Welfare Foundation reserves the right to approve or decline refund requests based on internal verification and applicable regulations.
